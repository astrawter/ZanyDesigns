A little about me...

My name is Alexandria and I am a Houston based graphic and web designer. I am currently working towards my degree in Web Design and Development at Full Sail University. I have always found joy in designing and chose to study in an environment that works with my schedule and allows me to expand my skill set. I look foward to helping manage social media sites, designing flyers and business cards, as well as creating the best web design to fit your needs. I am excited to update my portfolio as I create more projects and designs to showcase my abilities.
